My Intro

About me

Hello, I'm Ankit Kumar, a passionate Frontend Developer dedicated to crafting seamless digital experiences. Equipped with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Rabindranath Tagore University in Bhopal, my journey into the dynamic realm of technology has been both enlightening and exhilarating. With a wealth of experience as a Frontend Developer, I specialize in harnessing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to architect visually captivating and highly responsive user interfaces. Beyond coding, my skill set extends to integrating UI/UX design, ensuring that each element not only looks appealing but contributes meaningfully to an intuitive and enjoyable user experience.

My portfolio showcases a culmination of my skills and dedication to the craft. I am poised and enthusiastic about contributing my expertise to innovative projects, collaborating with dynamic teams, and making a meaningful impact in the digital sphere.
